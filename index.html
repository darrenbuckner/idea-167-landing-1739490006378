Executive Overview

An AI-driven platform that effortlessly cleans, deduplicates, and organizes data from multiple spreadsheets based on simple natural language commands, enabling teams to obtain accurate and ready-to-use lists in seconds.

Problem Statement

Teams managing contact lists, event attendees, or sales leads face the challenge of dealing with scattered, duplicate, and inconsistently formatted data across multiple spreadsheets, making manual cleaning time-consuming and error-prone.

Solution Statement

Our AI-powered platform automatically analyzes and cleans data from multiple spreadsheets by merging duplicates, filling in missing details, and organizing information based on natural language requests, saving time and ensuring data accuracy for better decision-making.
